Visual Concepts in Sketch-A-Net

An interactive exploration of visual concepts in Sketch-A-Net. Visual concepts derived using K-Means++ clustering (inspired by this paper).
Click on the visual concepts to see their top matches from the data and click on the top matches to compare activation patterns with the center of their visual concept cluster.
